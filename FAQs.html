FAQs

When is the RSVP deadline & what should I do if I have a dietary requirement? 

Please let us know about any dietary requirements on your RSVP form. If you have filled in that form you don't need to send us any more info.

Please RSVP before 1st December.

How do I get to the venue and where should I stay?  

Our brilliant venue (we love you Kate!) has prepared a really useful webpage on all the logistics for getting to and from the venue. It's linked here but do get in touch with us directly if you have any questions. There are suggestions for hotels and taxis on this page too if needed and there is free parking at the venue.

We suggest pre booking a taxi as the signal at Riverhill isn't amazing. Please note our wedding will finish at 11pm not midnight.

When should I arrive?

What should I wear?

Whatever makes you feel comfortable and most like yourself! This will be an outdoor wedding in the UK (sorry), so please do bear that in mind. You may want to factor in extra evening layers and also think about how your outfit and footwear will hold up on gravel, grass and during a Ceilidh.

Can we get you a gift? 

We aren't expecting you to get us a gift, as we appreciate that attending our wedding may already take up a lot of your time and money. However if you would still like to get us something then we'll be setting up a fund shortly where you can contribute. Watch this space!

 Can I bring a plus one?

We love you and we love your partner! We are also trying to celebrate with as many of our friends and family as possible, so are only able to welcome those named on your invite. Thank you for your understanding and we hope that you can join us on the day.
