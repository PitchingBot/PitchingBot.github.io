FAQs

Can I bring a plus one? 

We love you and we love your partner! We are also trying to celebrate with as many of our friends and family as we can afford, so are only able to welcome those named on your invite. Thank you for your understanding and we hope that you can join us on the day.

What should I wear? 

Whatever makes you feel comfortable and most like yourself! This will be an outdoor wedding in the UK (sorry), so please do bear that in mind. You may want to factor in extra evening layers and also think about how your outfit and footwear will hold up on gravel, grass and during a Ceilidh.

How do I get to the venue and where should I stay? 

Our brilliant venue (we love you Kate!) has prepared a really useful webpage on all the logistics for getting to and from the venue. It's linked here: https://www.riverhillgardens.co.uk/information-for-wedding-guests but do get in touch with us directly if you have any questions. We suggest pre booking a taxi as the signal at Riverhill isn't amazing. Please note: our wedding will finish at 11pm not midnight.

When is the RSVP deadline & what should I do if I have a dietary requirement? 

Please let us know on your RSVP form. If you have filled in that form you don't need to send us any more info.

Can we get you a gift? 

Firstly hi, thanks for clicking on this question you generous soul! We are very lucky to have an overly full flat and are also aware that attending a wedding is not cheap! It is also quite annoying when people don't want anything at all.

When should I arrive?
